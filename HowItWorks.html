How It Works:

1

Step 1: Fill out the Form

Start by filling out the carbon footprint calculator form. Provide information about your daily activities and consumption patterns that contribute to your carbon footprint.

2

Step 2: Calculate Your Carbon Footprint

Once you've provided all the necessary information, the calculator will process the data and calculate your carbon footprint. It takes into account various factors such as yearly mileage, electricity bills, and recycling of certain materials.

3

Step 3: Get Insights and Suggestions

After calculating your carbon footprint, you'll receive insights into your environmental impact. The system will provide personalized suggestions and recommendations to reduce your carbon footprint based on your results.
