Frequently Asked Questions & Help

What is EnvoGo and what does it do?

EnvoGo is a website that educates people about the harmful effects of carbon footprint, helps them calculate their daily carbon footprint, and suggests ways to reduce these harmful emissions.

How do I use the Calculator?

To use the calculator, fill out the necessary information prompted by the questions.

What is Carbon Footprint?

Carbon footprint is the total amount of greenhouse gases, such as carbon dioxide and methane, generated by our actions.

Is this Calculator Accurate?
